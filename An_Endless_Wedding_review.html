What is the Movie About?

The movie starts with Paul, a man who meets Louna, the sister of the bride, at a beautiful wedding taking place in a riad – a traditional Moroccan house – on the edge of the desert. Something strange happens during the wedding – a paranormal event occurs – and suddenly, they are trapped reliving the same day over and over again! They're stuck living the same wedding, with all its awkward moments and potential romance, for as long as it lasts.

As Paul and Louna try to figure out how to break free from this time loop, they start to fall in love. But can a love story really blossom when you know it’s going to repeat endlessly? It's a clever and charming question at the heart of the film!
