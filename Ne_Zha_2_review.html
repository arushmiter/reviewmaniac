Plot Summary & Story Breakdown

The story picks up immediately after the events of “Ne Zha” (the first movie). Ne Zha and Ao Bing have survived the Tribulation – a dangerous event that almost destroyed them. However, their physical bodies are still fading away, thanks to a powerful curse. The wise Taiyi Zhenren believes there’s hope: he has a magical lotus called the Seven Colored Lotus that can restore them! But getting this lotus isn't easy. It requires traveling across the seas and battling all sorts of monsters and mythical creatures.

The movie is packed with exciting quests. Ne Zha and Ao Bing sail to different oceans – the East Sea, the West Sea, and the North Sea – each guarded by a powerful Dragon King. They meet other legendary figures like Li Jing, who helps them fight, and Shen Gongbao, who provides support. Along the way, they learn more about their pasts and why they were cursed in the first place.

The main conflict comes from Ao Bing’s own desires. He wants to become a powerful being himself, even if it means going against Ne Zha's wishes. This creates tension and challenges their friendship as they try to find a way to complete their mission and save themselves.
