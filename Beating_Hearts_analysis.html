Clotaire’s choices lead him down a dark path involving violence and crime, separating him from Jackie for years.

Years later, both Clotaire and Jackie find themselves unexpectedly drawn back together. They discover that despite the time apart and the difficult circumstances surrounding their initial breakup, every step they’ve taken in life has ultimately led them back to each other.
